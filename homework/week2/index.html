Creative Computing


Homework #1 - The Human Computer

Create a webpage using HTML that contains a list of instructions meant to be executed by a human. Your instructions don't have to be for anything abstract (although I encourage you to be creative) - it can be as simple as "how to make a PB&J sandwich".


Requirements

  • Your code must at least contain the <html>, <head>, <title>, and <body> tags.
  • Your code must use the <ol> and <li> tags to create an ordered list of at least 3 steps in your instructions.

References

  • Marcel Duchamp - Unhappy Readymade
  • Sol LeWitt - Wall Drawing #65
  • Bruce Nauman - Body Pressure
  • Yoko Ono - Voice Piece for Soprano

Submission

You will submit your homework by uploading your HTML file to canvas.


Things To Think About

  • How can you be sure your instructions will be understood as you intended them to be?
  • What is the trade off between specificity and expression?
  • What is the relationship between the "instructor" and the "performer"?
